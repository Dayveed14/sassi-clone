WHO IS SASSI HOLFORD

Sassi Holford is an award-winning British fashion designer who has been creating bridal gowns, couture dresses and timeless evening wear for over 40 years. She aims to produce effortlessly elegant pieces that champion craftsmanship and are considerate of the environment. Every garment is handmade in England by Sassi and her incredible team of talented artisans. Find out more about the story behind the brand or read about our sustainability ethos.

Her iconic designs are renowned for their superior cut and fit, which have attracted many celebrity clients. This list includes Autumn Phillips’, who chose a Sassi Holford design for her royal wedding, Dame Helen Mirren, Shirley Ballas &

Marisha Wallace. Sassi’s also responsible for some of Holly Willoughby’s most memorable Dancing On Ice dresses. View our As Seen On gallery and magazine for more.

Let There Be Love, Sassi Holford’s 2023 collection, is the reimagining of modern femininity, pairing innovative fabrics with confident, clean silhouettes. The combination of organic textures, defined lines and intricate detail showcase Sassi’s unrivalled talent and instinctive understanding of the female form. Explore the 2023 Let There Be Love collection, find your nearest stockist or book an appointment at our London or Somerset boutique.
